Organization Annual Reports
Beaumont Grosse Pointe (BGP) no longer publishes a formal annual report. The information previously contained in the report is reviewed and documented at the December BGP Leader Meeting, which all hospital leaders and physicians are invited to attend. BGP also published a Facts and Figures sheet that is available on the public website and outlines:

  • Specialty Services
  • Distinctions
  • Awards and Rankings
  • 2016 Key Statistics
  • Community Involvement and Partnerships
