NK5EO

 

Innovation NK5EO

 

Nurses are involved with the design and implementation of technology to enhance the patient experience and nursing practice.

  • Provide one example, with supporting evidence, of an improvement that occurred due to a change in nursing practice resulting from clinical nurses' involvement with design and implementation of technology. Supporting evidence must be submitted in the form of a graph with a data table that clearly displays the data.
    AND

  • Provide one example, with supporting evidence, of an improvement in the patient experience that resulted from clinical nurses' involvement with design and implementation of technology. Supporting evidence must be submitted in the form of a graph with a data table that clearly displays the data.
