Beaumont

 


Dear Colleagues:

 

 

It is an honor and a privilege as the Vice President and Chief Nursing Officer of Beaumont Hospital Grosse Pointe to submit our document for the American Nurses Credentialing Center Magnet® Recognition Program. Beaumont Grosse Pointe has provided high-quality, compassionate healthcare for the community, especially those who are poor and the most vulnerable, for many years. As you read and review our document, you will see how Beaumont’s Mission, Vision, and Values drive the exceptional care provided by our nurses.

 

Nurses at Beaumont Grosse Pointe are empowered through shared governance and department-based activities to explore best practices and to question the “why” behind current practices through evidence-based research. This will be evident in our documents as well as in our overview of quality, safety, patient, and employee satisfaction.

 

This application reflects the outstanding work our nurses do every day and the leadership they exhibit at the bedside. I am proud to share and showcase their accomplishments through this electronic Document. 

 

 

Sincerely,

 

 

Anne M. Stewart, MBA, BSN, RN, NE-BC
